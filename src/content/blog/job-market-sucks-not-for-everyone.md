### About the Speaker

**Marianna Hrynyshyn** is a Senior Technical Recruiter at **TeamIT**, a Calgary-based agency placing tech talent across Canada and the US. With **10+ years of experience** hiring software engineers, AI, and product professionals globally, she knows exactly what makes a candidate stand out — and what gets them ignored.

Beyond recruiting, Marianna helps tech professionals position themselves for the North American market: sharpening their LinkedIn, telling their story with confidence, and landing roles that match their actual level.
